About Austin

Thomas Austin Barnes IV was born May 21, 2002 growing up in Idaho. Being born and raised there he attended Hillcrest High School graduating in 2020. Austin loves soccer, Star Wars, video games, and his family. Austin goes by his middle name as his dad and grandpa both share his name, both going by Thomas or Tom. During high school Austin would be doing one of three things, school, soccer, or video games. He spent most of that time playing soccer allowing him to play for his school and going to tournaments. When he had free time he liked to play games with his friends and family. Austin served his mission in the South Africa, Durban mission. Though this time was hard for him and he didn't complete the whole two years he still cherishes his time there and the people he met. Austin is attending Brigham Young University Idaho with Rachel, studying software engineering in hopes to become a software developer. Austin hopes to be a software engineer for a big company developing exciting software to better the world. He also hopes to start a family and have kids one day.
