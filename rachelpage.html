About Rachel

Rachel Hurst was born July 30, 2001 growing up in Nebraska. In her teens they moved to Idaho where she attended Skyline High School graduating in 2019. She works at the Children's Adventure Center, working with special-needs children helping them develop skills. Rachel loves, horses, cows, Harry Potter, and her family. She is a very outgoing person and is always cheerful even in hard circumstances. She is very loving and kind towards everyone. She works hard and plays harder being the ultimate source of energy. Rachel served a mission in the Birmingham, Alabama mission. She loves the people there and had a wonderful time serving the people and the Lord. She attends Brigham Young University Idaho with Austin, working on getting a early childhood special-needs education degree. So that she can continue to work with the great kids she loves. She hopes to one day be a stay at home mom with kids running around the home.
